Available Challenges

Super dooper cool coaster
Fast moving Roller-coaster with many loops.
Cool Current
A large, circular, cold river that you must cmplete a lap in.
Delicious Crunch
The delicious crunch is a food eating competition where contestants will eat very crunchy foods.

Safety precautions

Once every three months the park will be inspected to ensure safety of all viewers and contestants.

If there is danger in the park you must go to the evacuation spot near the Cool Current ride.

Ride Commands

Start the coaster: ride.start("Super dooper cool coaster")

Open the trail: move.current("Cool Current")

Start the Clock: start.timer("Delicious crunch")

In the park lingo

SDCC The shortened version of the most popular roller-coaster.

CCR are fun meetups in the Cool Current.
