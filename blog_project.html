The start of a healthy lifestyle

Why balanced meals matter

Having a healthy diet helps the brain focus enough to study, play, think, and grow.
In said diet you should have lots of vegetables, fruits, and grains.
Be sure to include water during your meal.

Learn more about healthy eating here: MyPlate Nutrition Guide

Healthy Snack options

Snacks can be healthy and delicious, all you have to do is use less sugar and add more benefitial nutrients.
Fruits, nuts, sprouts, and yoghurt are better choices than chips and sugary drinks.

Explore more nutrition facts here: WHO Healthy Diet Guide

Nutrition Tips for Students

Drink Enough Water

Water helps the body stay active, supports digestion, and keeps the brain focused. Students who know they are activie or do not have access to water throughout the entire day should carry a water bottle and drink water throughout the day.

Eat Colourful Food

Different colours in fruits and vegetables give different nutrients. A colourful plate often means a healthier plate.

Read more about healthy food choices here: Nutrition.gov

Quick Nutrition Note

A simple way to remember what types of food you need to insure a healthy diet is to fill half your plate with fruits and vegetables, one quarter with grains, and one quarter with protein.
