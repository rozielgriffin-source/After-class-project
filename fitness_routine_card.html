My Fitness Routine Cards

This page will give you an easy and simple way to work out and stay healthy. Follow these easy steps to get in shape without to much effort.

Warmups

Early morning stretches are good for your joints and get you prepared for the day.

Time: 10 minutes

Easy Movements

Going on early morning walks or runs is an easy and simple way to burn calories and stay fit.

Time: 30 minutes

Training Machines

Bench pressing, squating, and using dumbells are great for strength training.

Time: 25 minutes

Calisthenenics

Calisthenenics will help you strengten your body without equipment. Ex: pull ups, push ups, and sit ups.

Time: 20 minutes

Evening Walk

Go for a relaxing walk to stay active and refresh your mind.

Time: 25 minutes

Rest and Hydrate

Your workouts have to be fueled by energy. Drinking water will help you keep up during your workout.

Time: throughout the entire day

Working out and eating healthy are key factors in your mental, physical, and emotional health patterns.
